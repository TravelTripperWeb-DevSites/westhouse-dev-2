CHECK-IN

Check-in policy

A valid credit card must be presented upon arrival, along with a valid ID that matches the credit card presented.

Credit/debit card policy

Debit and credit cards will be authorized at check-in for the entire amount of your stay, plus $150 per day to cover incidentals. The system will hold the funds until check out, at which time the amount actually incurred during the stay will be charged. The original authorization amounts may take up to 30 days after departure to be released by your bank or financial institution and the hotel will not be responsible for any resulting fees or charges.

Check In/Check Out times

You may check in at 3:00 PM. Check Out is at 12:00 PM.

Age Restriction

Guests must be at least 21 years of age to check in at WestHouse. All other guests must be accompanied by a responsible adult.

OTHER POLICIES

Cancellation Policy

The hotel's 48-hour standard cancellation policy allows cancellation by 3 P.M. the day prior to arrival with no penalty. After this time, the credit card on file will be charged the first night's room rate plus taxes & fees. In the event you change your plans, you must cancel or change your hotel reservation in accordance with the hotel's cancellation policy to avoid penalties.

Smoking Policy

We are a non-smoking hotel. Penalty for smoking is a non-refundable charge of $500.

LOCATION AND TRANSPORTATION

Location

WestHouse is located at 201 West 55th Street, between 7th Avenue and Broadway.

Valet parking

We do offer Valet Parking only.

  • Sedan: $65.00*
  • Larger size/SUV: $75.00*

*Only valid for 24 hours parking per night of reservation.
*No in and out privileges

Airport Shuttle

We do not offer free shuttle service from the airport, but you are welcome to arrange transport ahead of time with our concierge by calling 212.707.4820.

HOTEL AMENITIES

Resident Fee

A nightly Residents Fee of $49.99 plus tax per adult is conveniently added to your folio and covers a vast array of bespoke, and unlimited, InResidence privileges:

  • Sweet and savory breakfast presentations
  • Barista bar and non-alcoholic beverages available all day
  • Hours of Indulgence, light fare and curated selection of alcoholic beverages served daily
  • High-speed Wi-Fi access
  • Local and toll-free phone calls
  • 24-hour Fitness Center, on the 3rd floor
  • 24-hour business center services
  • Bottled water in-room

**Please note, this fee is not inclusive of service gratuities.

Fitness Center

We have a full 24-hour Fitness Center on property, located on the third floor of our hotel, which includes state-of-the-art fitness bikes, treadmills, free weights, and weight machines.

Business Center

On The Terrace located on the 23rd floor, an iMac and printer are available for guests to view flights, check directions to various points of interests, and more.

Packages sent to the hotel

We accept packages that are sent to the hotel, which will be stored in our package room.

Luggage storage

Luggage can be stored on premises while rooms are being prepared, and after checkout until 9PM
